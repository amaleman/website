About

PhD candidate in Chemical Engineering at Stanford University working on electrocatalysis and sustainable energy technologies. My research focuses on understanding degradation mechanisms in cobalt-based materials using multimodal, time-resolved characterization techniques.

Research

PhD Research – Stanford University
Advisor: Prof. Thomas F. Jaramillo

Developing experimental platforms to monitor electrocatalytic activity, stability, and product generation in real time. Combining in situ and ex situ techniques (ICP-MS, EC-MS, AFM, XRD, synchrotron XAS) to study material degradation.
